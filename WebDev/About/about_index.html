About Us

Our Mission

To seamlessly translate the captivating world of Genshin Impact's Tarot into the digital realm. We aim to breathe life into these cards and creating an interactive experience. Our goal is to provide enthusiasts and newcomers alike with a dynamic, accessible platform to explore the mysteries of the Tarot, inviting them to embark on a digital journey through destiny and divination in the fantastical universe of Genshin Impact.

Founding Story

Our passion for the game ignited a spark of innovation. Fueled by the joy it brings us, we've resolved to craft a digital rendition. In the spirit of enhancing our experiences, we embark on this journey, ensuring that every draw, every prediction, remains as captivating as ever. This digital venture is a testament to our love for Genshin Tarot, a way to share its allure with fellow enthusiasts, and a tribute to the endless possibilities of virtual realms.

Future Vision

We aspire to recreate the essence of this game into a digital standalone board game. Our goal is ambitious yet clear: to immerse players in a virtual world where every card draw, every strategic move, echoes the magic of the original. Through innovative design, we weave a tapestry of adventure and mystery, inviting enthusiasts to embark on a digital journey unlike any other.

Theory crafting at the palm of your hand.
