THEORY CRAFTING AT THE PALM OF YOUR HAND

Hi, we are the Genshin Tarot RCOS team

Are you too lazy to do the math while you theory crafting your perfect deck in the critically acclaimed Genshin Tarot Board game? We are here to help you with that. Our goal is to allow you to do your own theory crafting quickly using our simulation.
